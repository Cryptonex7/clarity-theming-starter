Using CSS custom properties is the preferred approach to theming Clarity starting in version 3.0.0. After Clarity 4.0, it will be the only theming method that Clarity will support.
Toggling Stylesheets

An easy way to apply themes is to enable/disable stylesheets.

Here we have a toggle in the header that is enabling/disabling the dark theme. The approach illustrated is fairly tightly coupled to the order of the stylesheets as they are applied via webpack. There may be better approaches but the combination of webpack and Angular CLI pushed the demo in this direction.

This example is meant to illustrate one approach to dynamic theming. It may not constitute the best or recommended way for your particular use case.
